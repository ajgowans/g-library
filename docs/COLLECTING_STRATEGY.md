# Collecting Strategy

## Core

- Batman #404-574.
- Detective Comics #568-741.
- Complete Legends of the Dark Knight.
- Complete Shadow of the Bat.
- Strong focus on grounded Gotham, detective, gothic, crime and psychological stories.

## Facsimiles

Facsimiles are the preferred format for expensive pre-1986 keys where originals are unrealistic or poor value.

## Modern exceptions

- Gotham Central.
- Absolute Batman.
- Absolute Catwoman.
- Batman-focused Absolute one-shots and crossovers.

## Generally avoid

- Cosmic Batman.
- Heavy Justice League continuity.
- Multiverse/event-driven books unless they are truly exceptional.


## Facsimile strategy

Facsimiles are for issues where the original is either unrealistic, overpriced, or not desirable as a reading copy.

Default strategy:

- Golden Age keys: Facsimile only. Do not chase originals unless a miracle happens.
- Silver Age first appearances: Facsimile preferred, original only if unusually affordable.
- Bronze Age O'Neil/Adams and grounded Gotham classics: Facsimile preferred now, original maybe later.
- Post-Crisis issues: Original preferred if affordable; facsimiles are optional reading copies.

The key manual field is `facsimile_owned_confirmed`. The scripts can detect that you own a matching issue number, but they cannot reliably know whether that copy is original, facsimile, trade reprint, placeholder, or another reprint from the uploaded CSV alone.
